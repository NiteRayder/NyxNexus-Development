Overview

NyxEclipse is a Discord bot project developed under NyxNexus Development. The goal is to create a reliable, powerful, and expandable community management platform built around automation and intelligent systems.

The core Discord application, known as NyX-ES, serves as the foundation for future expansion into web and mobile platforms.

Planned Features

🛡 Moderation

Tools designed to help communities manage members, enforce rules, and maintain organization.

⚙ Server Management

Automation tools, configuration, utilities, and community systems.

🔔 Integrations

Future connections with platforms such as YouTube, Twitch, GitHub, and more.
